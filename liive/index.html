iPhone application

Liive helps entrepreneurs, by providing them financial indicators, to more easily manage their business. The application is connected to the company's bank account and allows more effective exchanges with their accounting firm.
During this three day assignment, we came up with some new features, designed a graphical interface and created a prototype based on static mockups.

Features

  • Esay-label

    Just a "swipe" gesture on a bank operation allows the user to convey the nature of the expenditure or of the receipt.

  • Anticipate

    The application provides the ability to quickly send proof of payment and automatically links to the relevant banking operation.

  • Clients

    The user can see the list of their clients and provide an update on their unpaid invoices. The connection to Linkedin displays additional information.

Prototyping

In addition to a mobile prototype created with Flinto, I chose to use Quartz Composer coupled with Origami to expose some more complex interactions and animations.
